# Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai

## Compute Infrastructure

- **Minerva HPC**: 25,000+ cores, 20+ PFLOPS, 452 TB RAM, 32 PB storage; LSF scheduler
- **Cloud**: UK Biobank RAP / DNAnexus (project-specific access)
- **Containers**: Docker (legacy), Singularity (HPC-compatible)

## Data Platforms

- **MSDW (Mount Sinai Data Warehouse)**: 12M+ patients, Epic-linked longitudinal EHR
- **AIR-MS (AI-Ready platform)**: Curated cohorts, structured for ML workflows
- **Mount Sinai BioMe Biobank**: 42,000+ participants; ~10,600 with WGS (TOPMed); ~38% Hispanic/Latino, 28% African American, 23% White; longitudinal EHR since 2003; dbGaP access phs001644/phs000925

## Core Facilities

- **Genomics Core**: NovaSeq X, PacBio Sequel II; directed by Robert Sebra
- **Proteomics and Metabolomics Core**: Discovery and Innovation Center; untargeted and targeted platforms
- **Microscopy and Advanced Bioimaging Core**: Confocal, super-resolution, lightsheet
- **Center for Bioinformatics**: Multi-omics integration support, pipeline development

## Research Centers (Relevant)

- Barbara and Maurice Deane Center for Wellness and Cognitive Health
- Alzheimer's Disease Research Center (ADRC) at MSSM
- Ronald M. Loeb Center for Alzheimer's Disease
- Friedman Brain Institute
- Institute for Regenerative Medicine

## Access Notes

- Minerva: institutional account required; PI sponsorship for lab members
- MSDW/AIR-MS: IRB approval + DUA required per project
- BioMe: controlled access; internal DUA for Mount Sinai; dbGaP DAR for external
- UK Biobank RAP: project-level application via DNAnexus; UK Biobank governance
