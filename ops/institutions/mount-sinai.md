## Compute Resources

**Minerva HPC** -- Primary research computing cluster at ISMMS. LSF scheduler, Rocky 9 nodes. ~24,000 cores, 210 TB memory, 20 GPU nodes, 32 PB storage, 11+ petaflops. InfiniBand interconnect. Open to all ISMMS investigators, postdocs, grad students, and Health System staff. Globus/GridFTP for data transfer, MySQL/MongoDB database hosting, TSM archival storage.

HPC filesystem path: `/sc/arion/projects/`

## Core Facilities

- **Flow Cytometry CoRE** -- 8 flow cytometers and 9 FACS machines across multiple campus locations. FlowJo analysis software. NIH Data Sharing-aligned reporting.
- **Genomics Core Facility** -- Short-read (Illumina: HiSeq2500 x2, MiSeq x2, NovaSeq x2, NextSeq550 x3, MiniSeq x2) and long-read (PacBio RSII, Sequel, Sequel II x2) sequencing. Directed by Robert Sebra, PhD.
- **Microscopy and Advanced Bioimaging Core** -- Widefield, laser scanning confocal, spinning disk confocal, multiphoton, light-sheet, super-resolution microscopes. 2 TEMs. High-content screening instruments.
- **Biorepository and Pathology CoRE** -- Biospecimen storage and pathology support services.
- **Mitochondrial Analysis Facility (MAF)** -- Agilent Seahorse XFe96, XFe24, XFp, HS Mini for real-time OCR and ECAR measurement. Standard assays: XF Glycolytic Rate, Cell Mito Stress, Cell Energy Phenotype Test. Directed by Jerry Chipuk. Training, collaborations, and consumables available.
- **Immunostaining Facility** -- Leica Bond RX for automated IHC, IF, and ISH. 30 slides per run. Frozen and FFPE compatible. Directed by Mihaela Skobe.
- **Laser Capture Microscopy** -- Fluorescent cell population micro-dissection from tissue sections. $25/hr investigator-operated or technologist charge-back.
- **Cancer Cell Organoid Core (CCOC)** -- Patient-derived human tumor lines from ISMMS clinical specimens. Currently breast tumor subtypes and stages.

## Platforms and Databases

- **Mount Sinai Data Warehouse (MSDW)** -- Data-intensive oncology and clinical research platform. Restricted-access procedures.
- **BiNGSSR** -- Bioinformatics for Next Generation Sequencing. Computational support for NGS, single-cell studies, and data visualization.
- **Human Immune Monitoring Center (HIMCSR)** -- High-throughput immune profiling and cellular characterization for immunotherapy research.
- **Cancer Genomics Technologies (CGTSR)** -- Bulk, single-cell, and spatial multi-omics for cancer research.
- **Scientific Computing and Data** -- 400+ researcher-controlled websites, 1,000+ managed application installations, MySQL/MongoDB database hosting, GridFTP/Globus data transfer, TSM archival storage.

## Shared Resources

- **Tisch Cancer Institute Shared Resources** -- NCI-Designated Comprehensive Cancer Center infrastructure. Seven shared core facilities supporting basic, translational, and clinical cancer researchers.
- **Dean's CoRES (Centers of Research Excellence)** -- Centralized shared facility network administered by the Office of the Dean. Fee-for-service or institutional subsidy model. Open to internal and external investigators.
- **Biostatistics (BSR)** -- Experimental design consultation, clinical trial support, statistical analysis, and comprehensive data science support.
- **Discovery and Innovation Center** -- 787 11th Ave, Midtown West (est. 2023). Expanded physical research infrastructure co-locating research groups with shared cores.
